# Pedagogy — Agent Instructions

## Purpose

This directory covers the pedagogical architecture and learning paths for
Prime Period Theory. It explains how the framework is taught, how learning
sequences are structured, and the cognitive moves defining PPT fluency.

Pages here bridge the theoretical foundations and the applications/implementations
layers, showing how the theoretical principles are translated into a learning path
and how tools should support this path.

## Tone guidance

Pedagogy pages should be expository, practical, and design-oriented. They describe
how to introduce concepts and why certain sequences or methods are preferred. Focus
on the learner's perspective and the cognitive shift required to understand PPT.
