# Amplitude Notation

> **Stub Notice:** This page represents an exploratory frontier of Prime Period Theory and is currently a stub.

## Overview

Standard music notation provides limited, subjective tools for amplitude (`p`, `mf`, `f`, accents). PPT seeks to formally extend its notation systems (Uniform Solfège, PPD, and MusiCoil) to capture exact dynamic trajectories and geometric amplitude ratios.
